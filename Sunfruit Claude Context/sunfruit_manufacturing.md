# Sunfruit Manufacturing & Operations Context

## Production Overview

Sunfruit produces organic powdered beverages in stick pack format. Current strategy uses contract manufacturing (co-packing) rather than in-house production.

---

## Stick Pack Specifications

### Physical Dimensions
- Width: 35mm
- Length: 105mm
- Fill weight: 5g powder

### Material Requirements
- **Priority**: Paper-based or eco-friendly (not single-use plastics)
- **Options**: FSC-certified paper, biolaminate, compostable laminate
- **Barrier**: Must protect against moisture for 18-24 month shelf life

## Pricing Expectations

### At Low Volume (1,000-5,000 units)
- Per stick pack: $0.40-0.60+
- Many manufacturers require 5,000-10,000 minimum
- Custom printed stick packs may require 20,000 unit MOQ

### At Scale (5M+ units/year)
| Component | Cost |
|-----------|------|
| Organic ingredients | $0.47 |
| Film/packaging | $0.015 |
| Filling | $0.032 |
| Overhead | $0.023 |
| **Total** | **$0.54** |

### Hitting $0.20-0.25 Target
Would require:
- Sacrificing organic certification, OR
- Reducing serving size to 3-3.5g, OR
- Switching to conventional ingredients

## Fulfillment Operations

### Self-Fulfillment (Current Strategy for Samples)
- Cost: $1.06 per sample package
- vs. FBA: $3.22 per package
- **Savings: $2.16 per sample**

### Sample Package Contents
- 3-4 stick packs
- A2 envelope (4 3/8" × 5 3/4")
- Insert card with offer/QR code
- Ships USPS letter rate (~$0.68)

### A2 Envelope Advantages
- Qualifies for USPS letter rate vs. flat rate ($0.95 savings per unit)
- Professional presentation
- Reliable machinability (no surcharge risks)
- Cost: ~$0.30-0.50 per envelope in bulk

## Facility Requirements (If In-House)

### Regulatory
- FDA registration as food manufacturing facility
- FSMA compliance (HARPC, Food Safety Plan)
- Good Manufacturing Practices (21 CFR Part 110/117)
- Allergen Control Program
- Labeling compliance

### Clean Room
- ISO 8 (Class 100,000) minimum
- Environmental monitoring for microbial contaminants
- Proper HVAC with filtration and positive pressure

### Equipment
- Form-Fill-Seal machinery for stick packs
- Powder handling (dust collection, sifting, blending)
- Quality control equipment

### Equipment Costs
- Single-lane stick pack machine: $50,000-$100,000
- Multi-lane systems: $150,000-$500,000+

## Supply Chain Considerations

### Ingredient Sourcing
- Lock in 2-year pricing with suppliers when possible
- Consider exclusive arrangements with organic suppliers
- Build relationships with multiple suppliers for redundancy

### Inventory Management
- Keep 6-8 weeks of inventory based on projected sales
- Account for seasonal demand fluctuations
- Stock deeper before marketing pushes

### Lead Times
- Initial formulation: 8-12 weeks
- Co-packer setup: 4-12 weeks
- Packaging prototypes: 2-4 weeks
- Production runs: 2-6 weeks depending on volume

---

## Cost Reduction Strategies

### Process Options
1. **Tolling** - You supply ingredients, they just package (cheapest)
2. **Full-service co-packing** - They source and produce
3. **Stock formulas** - Use pre-made formulations (saves R&D)

### Volume Leverage
- 5M+ units unlocks significantly better pricing
- Combine value brand + premium brand for volume
- Consider white-label or private label for additional volume

### Packaging Savings
- Stick packs use 15-50% less material than sachets
- Cylindrical design reduces film usage
- Negotiate on roll stock vs. pre-made pouches
