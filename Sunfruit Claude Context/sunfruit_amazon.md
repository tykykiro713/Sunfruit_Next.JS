# Sunfruit Amazon Strategy Context

## Positioning Strategy

### Critical Insight: You're NOT an Electrolyte Drink
Without sodium, Sunfruit is NOT competing with Liquid IV or LMNT. This opens up a different category with much less brutal competition.

### Primary Position: "Organic Water Enhancer"

**Hero Title Structure:**
"Sunfruit Organic Water Enhancer - Sugar Free Natural Fruit Flavoring Powder - No Sodium, Zero Calories, No Preservatives - Keto & Paleo Friendly - 30 Servings"

## Sample Pack Economics (Seller-Fulfilled)

### Unit Economics at $5
| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Revenue | $5.00 |
| Amazon Referral Fee (15%) | -$0.75 |
| Self-Fulfillment | -$1.06 |
| Product COGS (3 sticks) | -$0.75 |
| **Gross Profit** | **$2.44** |
| Amazon PPC (30% ACOS) | -$1.50 |
| **Net Profit** | **$0.94** |

**49% gross margin vs. 3% with FBA!**

### Why Self-Fulfill Wins
- FBA: 20% conversion × $0.13 profit = $0.026 per visitor
- Self-Fulfilled: 10% conversion × $0.94 profit = $0.094 per visitor
- **3.6x more profit per visitor despite algorithm penalty**

| ACoS (Month 2-3) | >60% | 35-60% | <35% |

| CPC | >$2.50 | $1.20-2.50 | <$1.20 |

### Action Triggers
- <0.3% CTR: Bad creative or targeting, fix main image
- >100% ACOS after 100 clicks: Pause keyword
- <25% ACOS: Increase budget 50%
- Conversion <7%: Test main image first (biggest impact)
