# Sunfruit Marketing & Customer Acquisition Context

## Strategic Overview

Sunfruit's go-to-market strategy focuses on high-ROI, capital-efficient channels suitable for a solo founder. Priority channels ranked by efficiency:

1. **Subscription Box Placements** - Wholesale revenue + customer acquisition
2. **Micro-Influencer Partnerships** - Authentic, affordable reach
3. **Email Marketing** - Owned audience building
4. **Strategic PR** - Earned media
5. **Content Marketing** - Long-term asset building

---

## Subscription Box Strategy

### Business Models (Know Before Pitching)

**1. Wholesale Purchase (IDEAL)**
- Box buys product at 40-60% off MSRP
- Net 30-60 payment terms
- Orders: 100-5,000 units
- **Profit**: Immediate revenue + customer acquisition

**2. Free Product (Selective)**
- You provide free product, they distribute
- Only worthwhile for: 10K+ subscribers, perfect audience fit
- Calculate: Subscribers × 10% conversion × LTV to determine ROI

**3. Pay-to-Play (Usually Avoid)**
- You pay $500-5,000 placement fee
- Skip unless: Under $10 CAC, or major box with 50K+ engaged subscribers

**4. Hybrid Models**
- Revenue share (20-30% commission)
- Consignment (pay after ship)
- Exclusive deals (free product + customer data)

### Priority Target Boxes

**Tier 1 - Perfect Fit Niche (Wholesale Buyers)**
- Keto Krate (~10K subscribers) - Already includes powder beverage mixes like Ultima
- PaleoLife Box - Net 30 terms
- Love With Food - Wholesale + donates meals
- Fit Snack
- Vegan Cuts

**Tier 2 - Emerging/Regional**
- Clean.Fit Box
- The Good Box
- Healthy Surprise Box
- Natural Wellness Box

**Tier 3 - Major Lifestyle (Complex/Selective)**
- FabFitFun
- Causebox/ALLTRUE

### Wholesale Deal Economics Example
- Box orders 500 units
- Your retail: $20
- Wholesale (50% off): $10
- Your COGS: $3
- **Profit: $3,500 + 500 potential new customers**

## Sampling Strategy

### Self-Liquidating Offer (SLO) Model

**Sample Pack Economics:**
- 3-4 stick packs per sample
- Customer pays $5 shipping
- Your COGS + fulfillment: ~$1.90
- **Net margin for CAC: $3.10**

**Conversion Funnel (Conservative):**
- Cold traffic → Sample: 10%
- Sample → Purchase: 10%
- Purchase → Subscribe: 20% (2% of sample recipients)

### Target CAC for Samples
- **Sweet spot: $4.50-$5.50 per sample sale**
- At $5 CAC: Net profit of $14.31 per sample (250-350% ROI)
- Allows 70% reinvestment into ads = 70% month-over-month growth

### Sample Distribution Channels

**Direct Mail**
- EDDM (Every Door Direct Mail): $0.24-$0.29 per piece
- Target high-income zip codes (Cary, NC 27511 example: $104K median income, 13,684 households)
- Expected response rate for prospects: 2-4%
- Personalization can more than double response

**Subscription Boxes**
- Cost: $0.50/placement (2 samples @ $0.25 each)
- Typical conversion to website: 5-8%
- Sample → Purchase rate: 10-20%
- **Projected CAC: ~$20** with high LTV:CAC ratio

### Sample-to-Purchase Conversion Optimization
- Include branded insert card driving tin purchases
- QR code linking to 20% off landing page
- Email capture for "flavor guide"
- UTM tracking: `utm_source=mail-insert&utm_medium=qr-code&utm_campaign=beverage-sample-20off-2025`

## Alternative Acquisition Channels

### SiriusXM Radio (Potential Winner)
- CPM: $1-2 (10-50x cheaper than Pandora/Spotify)
- Estimated CAC: $8-15
- Reaches 34M+ affluent subscribers
- "Sunfruit" is audio-friendly and memorable
- Minimum spend: ~$10,000 weekly

### Affiliate Marketing (Zero Risk)
- Only pay for actual conversions
- Typical commission: 10-20% of sale price
- For $25 product: CAC of $2.50-5.00
- No upfront costs or retainers

### Podcast Advertising
- Target health/wellness shows
- Host-read ads perform best
- Consider: The Model Health Show, Bulletproof Radio, Keto Diet Podcast

### Remnant/Local Cable TV
- Partner with remnant buyer (InfoWorx, Ho Hum Media)
- Test networks: Lifetime, HGTV, Food Network
- Weekday mornings + late night
- CPM: $1-3, CPA: $2.50-4
