**One Fruit + One Botanical** - Each Sunfruit flavor pairs a single organic fruit with a single organic botanical for a sophisticated, clean-label product.

## Formulation Notes by Flavor

### Raspberry Hibiscus
- **Status:** Gold standard achieved in Round 1 botanical powder version
- **Notes:** Best flavor depth with botanical powder vs extracts
- **Goal:** Replicate Round 1 profile

### Lemon Mint
- **Target:** Lemonade-level tartness
- **Feedback:** Increase citrus presence, decrease mint dominance
- **Position:** Classic, refreshing, gateway flavor

### Blueberry Lavender
- **Issue:** Lavender presence diminished in later iterations
- **Action:** Increase lavender while maintaining balance
- **Position:** Calming, wellness-oriented

### Pomegranate Rose
- **Status:** Round 1 powder version was ideal
- **Goal:** Match Round 1 profile with extracts if possible
- **Position:** Most sophisticated, elegant

## Product SKU Strategy

### 3+1 Strategy
Launch with 3 individual flavors + 1 Variety Pack (4 SKUs total, only 3 production runs)

**Individual SKUs:**
1. Raspberry Hibiscus - 30 count
2. Lemon Mint - 30 count
3. Blueberry Lavender - 30 count

**Variety Pack:**
- 30 count (10 of each flavor)
- Same price as single flavors
- Best for new customers

### Why 3 Individual Flavors Works
- Retail math: Buyers often say "pick your best 2-3"
- Consumer psychology: 3 choices optimal (not overwhelming)
- Marketing: "Each one is special" (not filling slots)
- Amazon: 3-packs ship better than 4-packs
- Sampling: Can afford more samples with fewer SKUs

## Flavor Testing Framework

### Before Committing to New Flavor
1. **Internal taste test** - Does it match brand vision?
2. **Spa water test** - "Would this taste good in spa water?"
3. **Consumer survey** - MaxDiff or preference ranking
4. **Small batch production** - 100-500 units for beta testing
5. **Subscriber feedback** - Offer to existing customers first

### Success Criteria for New Flavor
- >60% selection rate in MaxDiff testing
- Fills a gap in current lineup
- Fits fruit + botanical concept
- Achievable with organic ingredients
- Differentiated from competitors

## Competitive Flavor Landscape

### What Competitors Offer
- **Liquid IV:** Lemon Lime, Strawberry, Passion Fruit, Watermelon, etc.
- **Crystal Light:** Lemonade, Raspberry Lemonade, Peach Tea, etc.
- **MiO:** Berry, Lemonade, Tropical (synthetic flavors)
- **True Lemon:** Citrus-focused (lemon, lime, grapefruit, orange)

### Sunfruit's Differentiation
- Organic certification (unique in category)
- Fruit + botanical pairing (sophisticated concept)
- Real fruit taste (not artificial)
- Zero sodium (competitors have 500-1000mg)
- Clean label (3-5 ingredients)
