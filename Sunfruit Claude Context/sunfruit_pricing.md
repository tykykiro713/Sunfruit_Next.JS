## Subscription Discount Strategy

### Why 40% Works
- 15% discount = "Nice to have"
- 25% discount = "Good deal"
- **40% discount = "I'd be stupid NOT to subscribe"**

### Customer Psychology
```
One-time: $36.99
Subscribe: $21.97
SAVINGS: $15.02/month = $180/year
```
The 40% gap makes subscription the obvious choice.

### Margin Still Supports Growth
- Subscription gross margin: 56%
- At $3 CAC (from samples), profitable from Month 1
- LTV compounds with retention

## Payment Processing

### Shopify Payments Standard
- 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- On $5 sample: $0.45 (9% effective rate)
- On $36.99 one-time: $1.37 (3.7% effective rate)

### Impact on SLO
The $0.30 flat fee is painful on low-value transactions like $5 samples. At higher AOV, processing costs become negligible percentage.

At $5/sample CAC with 250-350% ROI:
