# Sunfruit Vine Section - SEO Content Strategy

## Overview
This document outlines the pillar and cluster content strategy for the Sunfruit "vine" section - a comprehensive health and wellness content hub that supports our healthy powdered beverage brand while providing valuable information to our target audience.

## Content Strategy: Pillar & Cluster Model

### What are Pillar Pages?
Comprehensive, authoritative pages (3,000-5,000 words) that cover broad topics in-depth, serving as the "hub" for a topic cluster.

### What are Cluster Pages?
Focused pages (1,500-2,000 words) that dive deep into specific subtopics, all linking back to their pillar page.

### SEO Benefits
- **Topic Authority** - Search engines recognize expertise on broad topics
- **Internal Linking** - Strong link structure boosts all pages in the cluster
- **Keyword Coverage** - Pillars target broad keywords, clusters target long-tail keywords
- **User Experience** - Easy navigation between related content

## Content Structure

### URL Structure
```
/vine/[pillar-topic] (Pillar Page)
├── /vine/[pillar-topic]/[cluster-1] (Cluster Page)
├── /vine/[pillar-topic]/[cluster-2] (Cluster Page)
└── /vine/[pillar-topic]/[cluster-n] (Cluster Page)
```

### Example Structure
```
/vine/diabetic-beverages (Pillar Page)
├── /vine/diabetic-beverages/reading-labels (Cluster)
├── /vine/diabetic-beverages/sugar-alternatives (Cluster)
├── /vine/diabetic-beverages/recipes (Cluster)
└── /vine/diabetic-beverages/blood-sugar-impact (Cluster)
```
