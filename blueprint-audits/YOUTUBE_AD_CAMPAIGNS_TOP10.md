## 1. First-Person Testimonial from Diabetics (Highest ROAS Potential)

### Why It's #1:
- **Specific audience pain point** (blood sugar management)
- **Strong social proof** with real testimonials
- **Visual proof** with glucose monitor
- **High purchase intent** from health-conscious audience

### Storyboard (60-second version):
- **0-3s:** Close-up glucose monitor showing spike, text overlay: "This used to be my nightmare"
- **3-8s:** Quick cuts of 3 diabetics introducing themselves
- **8-15s:** Person 1: "I've tried every sugar-free drink..."
- **15-25s:** Person 2: "Sunfruit is the first that actually tastes good"
- **25-35s:** Person 3 mixing Sunfruit, drinking, genuine smile
- **35-45s:** Split screen: glucose monitor staying flat + person enjoying drink
- **45-50s:** All 3 holding different flavors: "Finally, a drink we can enjoy"
- **50-60s:** Logo + "Try risk-free with our 30-day guarantee" + website

### Targeting Strategy:
- **Primary:** Custom intent audiences searching "diabetic friendly drinks," "sugar free beverages," "blood sugar control"
- **Secondary:** In-market for health & wellness products
- **Lookalike:** Based on current diabetic customers
- **Demographics:** 35-65, household income $50k+
- **Exclusions:** Current customers

### Bid Strategy:
- Start with **Target CPA** at $40 (2x current CAC)
- After 50 conversions, switch to **Target ROAS** at 400%
- Budget: $200/day initial test

### Production Requirements:
- **Cast:** 3 real diabetic customers (authenticity crucial)
- **Equipment:** iPhone 14 Pro + gimbal for intimate feel
- **Props:** Glucose monitors, Sunfruit products
- **Location:** 3 different home kitchens
- **Post:** Quick cuts, clear audio, captions
- **Budget:** $2,000-3,000

## Implementation Roadmap

### Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2): Test High-ROAS Concepts
- Launch #1 (Diabetic Testimonials) and #3 (3 Things I Hate)
- $300/day combined budget
- Establish baseline metrics

### Phase 2 (Weeks 3-4): Competitive & Educational
- Launch #2 (LMNT Attack) and #4 (Sodium Education)
- $300/day combined budget
- Test competitive messaging

### Phase 3 (Weeks 5-6): Lifestyle & Viral
- Launch #5 (Farmers Market) and #6 (ASMR)
- $200/day combined budget
- Build brand awareness

### Phase 4 (Week 7+): Scale Winners
- Double down on top 3 performers
- Test remaining concepts based on learnings
- Scale budget to $1,000+/day

### Success Metrics:
- **Primary:** ROAS >300%, CAC <$40
- **Secondary:** View rate >25%, CTR >1%
- **Brand:** Lift in branded searches, social mentions

### Testing Variables:
- Video length (15s, 30s, 60s versions)
- Thumbnail options (3 per video)
- Opening hooks (test multiple)
- CTAs (discount vs. free shipping vs. guarantee)
